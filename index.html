Australia Green Energy Analysis

This dashboard presents an analysis of Australia's green energy landscape in 2023 (Financial Year). The visualizations below illustrate the relationship between CO2 emissions and energy generation across different states, as well as the renewable energy rates in each state.

Energy Generation vs CO2 Emissions

This scatter plot shows the correlation between total energy generation (in GWh) and CO2 emissions from energy (in Mt) for each state. Each point represents a state, labeled with its abbreviation.
